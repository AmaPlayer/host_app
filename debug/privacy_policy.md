## Introduction

Welcome to **AmaPlayer**, a sports social media platform that connects athletes, coaches, organizations, and fans. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using AmaPlayer, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account and use AmaPlayer, we collect:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
- **Profile Information:** Sports profile, position, team, achievements, bio
- **Content:** Posts, photos, videos, comments, messages, stories
- **Verification Data:** Documents or videos submitted for verification
- **User Connections:** Information about connections and interactions between users (message content is not tracked)

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our platform, we automatically collect:

- **Usage Data:** Pages viewed, features used, time spent, interactions
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
- **Location Data:** Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS)
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** See Section 4 below

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

- **Social Media:** If you connect your social media accounts
- **Analytics Providers:** Usage statistics and performance data
- **Advertising Partners:** Ad performance and engagement data

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 Public Information

The following information is **publicly visible** by default:

- Your profile (name, photo, bio, sports information)

- Your comments on public content
- Your followers and following lists

- **Firebase/Supabase:** Database and authentication services

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### 3.4 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

- Legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
- Government requests
- Protection of our rights and safety

### 3.5 Business Transfers

If AmaPlayer is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 4. Cookies and Advertising

### 4.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our services.

### 4.2 How We Use Cookies

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for the platform to function (login, security)
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with our platform
- **Advertising Cookies:** Used by Google AdSense and other ad networks to show personalized ads

### 4.3 Google AdSense

We use **Google AdSense** to display advertisements on our platform. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to:

- Show personalized ads based on your interests
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud

**Your Choices:**
- You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)
- You can learn more about how Google uses data at [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

### 4.4 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our platform.

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your information at any time through your account settings.

### 5.2 Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time. This will permanently remove:

- Your profile information
- Your posts, photos, and videos
- Your messages and comments

Note: Some information may be retained for legal or security purposes.

### 5.3 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a portable format.

### 5.4 Opt-Out of Marketing

You can opt out of marketing communications by:

- Clicking "unsubscribe" in emails
- Adjusting notification settings in your account

### 5.5 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights:

- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

### 5.6 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- Know what personal information we collect
- Know whether we sell or share your personal information
- Opt out of the sale of your personal information
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

**We do not sell your personal information.**
